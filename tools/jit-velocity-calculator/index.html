JIT Velocity Calculator

Human access volume and NHI credential issuance have very different throughput requirements. A JIT architecture sized for 200 engineers may not handle 50,000 NHI token issuances per day without structural changes. Enter your access volumes and latency tolerance to get a deployment blueprint describing which architecture category fits, not a single vendor pick, since the right product within a category still depends on your specific stack.
